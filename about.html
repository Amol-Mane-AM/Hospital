About Us

A Legacy of Healing & Compassionate Care

Our Journey

Established in 1998, Mahatma Gandhi Hospital has been at the forefront of medical excellence. What started as a modest 50-bed healthcare facility has grown into a premier multi-speciality institution with over 500 beds, serving thousands of patients across the region.

Our philosophy has always been simple: treat every patient like family. Over the decades, we have continuously upgraded our infrastructure, embraced advanced medical technologies, and brought in some of the brightest medical minds to deliver world-class care.

Today, we stand as a beacon of hope and healing, committed to our founding principles of integrity, affordability, and uncompromising quality.
